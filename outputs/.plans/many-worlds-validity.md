# Research Watch Plan: Many-Worlds Validity

## Topic
Real-time research papers enhancing the sophistication and validity of the Many-Worlds interpretation (MWI) in the fields of quantum mechanics and quantum cosmology.

## What to Monitor
- New preprints and peer-reviewed papers on arXiv (quant-ph, gr-qc, hep-th) and major physics journals.
- Theoretical advancements in Everettian quantum mechanics, decoherence, and quantum cosmology models involving multiple branches or universes.
- Potential experimental proposals seeking to differentiate MWI from other interpretations (e.g., macroscopic superposition tests).

## Key Signals
- New mathematical frameworks making MWI more rigorous (sophistication).
- Proposed or executed experiments addressing the probability problem (Born rule) or preferred basis problem in MWI.
- Cosmological models integrating MWI with quantum gravity or early universe inflation.

## Meaningful Change
- Publication of a paper proposing a testable prediction unique to MWI.
- Major theoretical consensus or breakthrough resolving the Born rule derivation.
- Widespread citation of a new MWI quantum cosmology framework.

## Check Frequency
Weekly.
