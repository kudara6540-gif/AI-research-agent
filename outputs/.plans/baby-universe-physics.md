# Watch Plan: Baby Universe (Physics)

## Objective
Monitor and aggregate new research papers, theoretical developments, and observational proposals related to the concept of "baby universes" within theoretical physics and cosmology.

## What to Monitor
- **Primary Sources**: arXiv preprints (hep-th, gr-qc, astro-ph), alphaXiv, published papers in major physics journals (PRD, PRL, JHEP).
- **Keywords**: "baby universe", "multiverse", "third quantization", "wormhole baby universe", "quantum cosmology baby universes", "Coleman's baby universe mechanism".

## Signals That Matter
- **Theoretical Developments**: New mathematical frameworks involving string theory, loop quantum gravity, or third quantization that incorporate baby universes.
- **Observational Signatures**: Proposals for detecting signatures of baby universes in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) or stochastic gravitational wave background.
- **Topological Interpretations**: Novel insights into spacetime topology fluctuations (spacetime foam) and wormhole physics linking to baby universes.

## Meaningful Change
- A highly cited preprint proposing a new, testable mechanism.
- Experimental/observational papers constraining models that feature baby universes.
- Shifts in theoretical consensus around the cosmological constant problem via Coleman's mechanism or modern equivalents.

## Check Frequency
- **Weekly** sweep of new papers and preprints.
