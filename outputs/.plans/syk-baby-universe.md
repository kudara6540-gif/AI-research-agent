# Watch Plan: SYK Models & Baby Universe Implementations

**Objective:**
Monitor new theoretical models, exact implementations, and numerical results connecting the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev (SYK) model to the emergence of "baby universes" and holographic wormholes. 

**Monitoring Scope:**
1. **Primary Sources:** arXiv (hep-th, quant-ph), major journals (JHEP, PRD).
2. **Keywords:** "SYK model baby universe", "DSSYK baby universes", "Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev wormholes", "double-scaled SYK matrix models".

**What Constitutes a Meaningful Change (Signal):**
- **New Mathematical Constructions:** Papers proposing novel operators, Hamiltonians, or matrix models that generate baby universes (e.g., similar to the "ETH matrix model of double-scaled SYK").
- **Holographic Advances:** New 2D or 3D holographic gravity duals demonstrating closed "baby" universes emerging from thermal pure states in SYK.
- **Experimental/Quantum Simulation:** Proposals for simulating SYK-based baby universes on near-term quantum hardware (rare, but high-impact).
- **Code/Implementation:** Any codebase published alongside papers that numerically solve DSSYK Hamiltonians with baby universe operators.

**Check Frequency:** 
Weekly sweeps.

**Review Artifacts:**
Append new papers and updates to `outputs/syk-baby-universe-baseline.md` (or a dedicated watch log) and log in `CHANGELOG.md`.
