# Research Watch Plan: Baby Universe Theory in Quantum Cosmology

## What to Monitor
- ArXiv repositories (`gr-qc`, `hep-th`, `astro-ph.CO`) for new preprints.
- AlphaXiv discussions and latest physics journals.
- Keywords: "baby universe", "third quantization", "spacetime wormholes", "quantum cosmology", "topology change", "multiverse".

## Signals That Matter
- Theoretical advancements reinforcing the validity or mathematical sophistication of baby universe models.
- Connections between baby universes and black hole information paradox resolutions.
- Integrations with major quantum gravity frameworks (String Theory, Loop Quantum Gravity).
- Any proposed observational signatures (e.g., CMB anomalies, gravitational wave backgrounds).

## What Counts as a Meaningful Change
- Publication of highly cited or peer-reviewed papers providing new mathematical proofs.
- Introduction of novel cosmological models that solve existing tension (like the Hubble tension) using baby universes.
- Paradigm shifts in how baby universes interact with the parent universe.

## Check Frequency
- Weekly recurring scan for new papers and developments.
